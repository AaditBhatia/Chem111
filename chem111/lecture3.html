  • Converting between Units.
    48hours=? minutes
    Step 1 : Write the quantity in curr units and identify the desired new units.
    48 hours x conversion factor = ? minutes
    Step 2: Relate curr units and desired units by known equality and write it as a ration. 1 hour = 60 minutes. 1 minute= 1/60 hours, 1 hour= 60/1 minutes
    Step 3 : Multiply original value by conversion factor such that original units are cancelled by same units in the denominator. 48 hours x 60 minutes/ 1 hour=> the hour units get cancelled off. 48 hours= 48 x 60 = 2880 minutes
